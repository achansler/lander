How It Works

Join The Slack Team

Answer a few questions for us so we can get to know you, and we'll send you a Slack invitation.

Post What You're Working On

Upload whatever you're working on and let everyone know a bit about it.

Get & Give Feedback

Get feedback on your designs and give feedback to others!

Some of Our Channels

Upload your work in any stage to the channels. We love to see every part of the process, from initial sketches to that final_final_reallyFinal.psd.

#intros

When you first join the team, introduce yourself here! Tell us whatever you want (hopes, dreams, favorite color...)

#logos

That one word or symbol that represents an entire brand (no pressure) could probably use another set of eyes.

#apps

Working on the next big app, but something just isn't right? We've been there. Post some screens here.

#branding

There are so many elements to branding work. Make sure you thought of everything here.

#print

This channel is for getting feedback on anything from business cards to posters.

#websites

Got visual designs for a website? Wireframes? Sketches?! If you want feedback on anything web related, this is the channel for you.
